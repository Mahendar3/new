1. Home:

Rohit Sharma is a renowned Indian international cricketer and the captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats. Known for his elegant batting style, Rohit has broken numerous records in One Day Internationals (ODIs) and T20 cricket.

Born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, Rohit is one of the most prolific run-scorers and is famous for his multiple double centuries in ODIs.

Career Highlights

  • ODI Double Centuries – Holds the record for the highest number of double centuries in ODIs.
  • IPL Success – Captain of Mumbai Indians, leading them to multiple IPL titles.
  • ICC T20 World Cup – Key player in India's ICC T20 World Cup campaigns.
  • Test Cricket – Strong performances in Test matches, especially in overseas tours.
  • Captaincy – Renowned for tactical acumen and leadership skills in limited-overs cricket.

Achievements and Records:

1. Highest Individual Score in ODIs:

Why it’s famous: Rohit holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs with 264 runs.

Where it happened: Against Sri Lanka in 2014.

Fact: This innings included 33 fours and 9 sixes.

2. IPL Captaincy:

Why it’s important: Led Mumbai Indians to 5 IPL championships, making them the most successful IPL team.

Fun fact: Rohit is one of the few captains with multiple IPL titles.

3. Multiple ODI Double Centuries:

Why it’s important: First player to score three double centuries in ODIs.

Fun fact: Known as “Hitman” for his big-hitting style.
